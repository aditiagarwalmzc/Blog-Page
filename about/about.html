Who is Mig, you ask?

So who really am I?

I am Aditi, an 18 yo student, better known as Mig Z. Captain. This is my pen name. If you're wondering, "Why this name?" or "What in the world does this name mean?" then let's just say, I like The Kite Runner and Dead Poets Society, a little too much. Out of all the things I occasionally enjoy, my music taste (mostly Indie/Alt Rock) is something I'm abnormally proud of! Recently, I have developed an interest in development and programming, so I thought "Hey! What if I combined two of my most beloved interests, programming and writing?" Hence, this website!


Why did I start this blog?

After I realized that my interest lies in technology, I started developing stuff on my own. Google has been my best friend ever since. (Which almost makes me wanna forgive it for stealing so much of my data.) The Internet community has helped me a lot through this journey and every once in a while, I would come across a blog post that would not only answer my question but would also give me a sense of being normal, in this otherwise loophole of insanity, when I find something I can relate to! This site is simply an attempt to put my ideas and words out there for someone who can one day, hopefully, relate to.


What will I write?

I will try to pen down new things I have discovered or perhaps an interesting piece of information, not just limited to technology but personal interests too. (Say, I come across a field of Daffodils or a maiden singing in the field? O.o) I may also post my poems here, when/if I'm ready to put them out there for the world. Until then, happy reading!
