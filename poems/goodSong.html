A Good Song

I heard a good song, what do I do?
Do I sing it in my horrendous voice,
Am I even supposed to?
Delightful, terrible ways - I will allow,
I will gulp it down for days, but how?

I will set it on fire, if only could I
I will kiss it in the face,
I choose not to answer why.
But where am I now? Is it those pills?
Up in the space, ‘high’ above those hills?

I will listen on a loop now,
It will pass like a hazy phase.
But the music's so loud here
To make it matter anyways.
