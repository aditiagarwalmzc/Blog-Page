Need I Justify?

Dare my shoes walk me
out of this lifestyle.
Where lively seems the loneliness
and jolly, juvenile.

I stay for hours in my shrine,
not knowing what to do.
I pick my quill, I try to write,
not knowing what to.

I get reviled for being idle
when I was thinking thoughts.
But how do I really justify
the process of connecting dots.
